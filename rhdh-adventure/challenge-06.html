ğŸ”Œ Unlock New Capabilities

Imagine unlocking a whole new world of possibilities for your Red Hat Developer Hub with just a few steps!

This challenge is all about tapping into the power of extendibility. By leveraging plugins, you can enhance the functionality of your Red Hat Developer Hub, tailoring it to better meet user needs and creating a more dynamic and engaging experience.

In this challenge, you’ll explore how to integrate a plugin into the Red Hat Developer Hub. Plugins provide a seamless way to extend the Red Hat Developer Hub’s capabilities without modifying its core code, offering flexibility and scalability for future enhancements. With a wide array of available plugins, this activity encourages you to explore and expand the application’s feature set to make it even more powerful.

The challenge will be considered successful when:

  • A plugin is added to the Red Hat Developer Hub instance without issues.

  • The functionality of the plugin is accessible and usable by the end user.

By the end of this challenge, you’ll understand the importance of extendibility of Red Hat Developer Hub and gain hands-on experience with integrating plugins to enhance functionality.
